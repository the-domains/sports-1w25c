Intro

Hey guys! Finally have a site up and running to post some picks! I’m a model MLB bettor meaning I built a model to predict the outcome of MLB games. I will only place a bet if I have an edge over the line. My bets range from 1u to 10u. A play over 2u is very rare. It is always best to use a 100u bankroll, but a 50u bankroll should be large enough for my style of betting. All bets will be placed on the ML. All picks are posted to Twitter @MLBettor.
